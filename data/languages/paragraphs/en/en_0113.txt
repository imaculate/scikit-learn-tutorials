In addition to logistic growth in the number of its articles,[290] Wikipedia has steadily gained status as a general reference website since its inception in 2001.[291] About 50% of search engine traffic to Wikipedia comes from Google,[292] a good portion of which is related to academic research.[293] The number of readers of Wikipedia worldwide reached 365 million at the end of 2009.[294] The Pew Internet and American Life project found that one third of US Internet users consulted Wikipedia.[295] In 2011 Business Insider gave Wikipedia a valuation of $4 billion if it ran advertisements.[296]
